2. About the App

Flight Path is a training companion app designed for students undertaking the United Kingdom Private Pilot Licence (Aeroplane) — PPL(A) — qualification. The App provides structured support for the 19 exercises outlined in the CAA's CAP 1298 syllabus, including pre-lesson preparation tools, post-lesson debriefs, progress tracking, and AI-assisted learning aids.

Flight Path is intended solely for use by student pilots enrolled in, or preparing for, UK PPL(A) training. The App is designed to complement — not replace — formal flight instruction from a qualified instructor.
